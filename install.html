Install LIV Intel Social

Add to your home screen for app-like access

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1
Open this page in Safari (not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button (square with arrow, bottom toolbar)
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — the LIV Social icon appears on your home screen

Android (Chrome)

1
Open this page in Chrome
2
Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
3
Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4
Tap Add — open like any app from your home screen
